I write to organize my thoughts, grow my vocabulary, and document what I learn—always responsibly, without sharing sensitive data. Editorial transparency & automation. I choose the topics and write the first draft. Then a workflow with integrated AI (via API) suggests improvements to spelling, style, and clarity, runs basic checks (metadata/SEO and links), and prepares the post for publishing. I review every change and approve it before anything goes live on mikai.blog. Automation speeds me up, but it never replaces my voice. Philosophy of mikai.blog. Not everything has an immediate answer, and the future is uncertain. I prefer steady iteration over perfect-once: publish, learn, improve—while keeping the human touch.
